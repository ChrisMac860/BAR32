Welcome to Bar 32 – Traditional Irish Bar

About

Bar 32 is a traditional Irish bar located in the heart of Torrevieja, Alicante, offering an authentic taste of Ireland with a warm and friendly atmosphere. Known for having the best Guinness in the La Zenia and Torrevieja region, Bar 32 combines classic Irish hospitality with scenic waterfront views and lively entertainment. Whether you're after a relaxed drink, hearty Irish fare, or a night of live music and sports, Bar 32 welcomes you to experience a true Irish pub away from home.
